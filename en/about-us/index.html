About us

Who are we?

Pro Sistem

Pro sistem d.o.o. is a young and dynamic company that is its own work started in 2022, with headquarters in Podgorica. Our team consists of trained experts with many years of experience in the industry ventilation and air conditioning, providing superior solutions that meet the various needs of clients in Montenegro and beyond.

Without regardless of whether it is residential ventilation and air conditioning or business premises, as well as industrial facilities, our team of experts is dedicated to providing the best possible solutions, leading takes into account energy efficiency.

Aware that every space has our specific requirements, we approach each project with care and by understanding the client's needs. We offer personalized solutions which are adapted to specific conditions and goals, regardless of size and type of object.

Quality as an imperative of our business, is reflected in cooperation with leading brands in the industry, and using the latest technologies and innovations. Regardless of project size, Pro sistem d.o.o. always approaches with the same enthusiasm and professionalism to ensure exceptional results.

Our support does not end after installation. Regularly we provide maintenance services to ensure long-lasting and optimal operation of our systems. Fast and efficient customer support it is the foundation of the long-term partnership relationships we build.
