Why does it matter?

Ventilation systems maintain air quality, regulate humidity and temperature, and help to remove unpleasant odors from space. The main purpose of ventilation is to improve quality internal air by replacing it. There are different types system, including natural, mechanical and balanced ventilation. The role of ventilation systems is not only technical, but is also key to creating a comfortable and healthy interior space. In addition to efficiency, ventilation systems today are an indispensable part of the interior of spaces that have a commercial purpose. Proper selection and maintenance of ventilation systems are key to long-term effectiveness.
